1. Application Form(signed and scanned): This implies pages 1 & 2 of the downloaded pdf of the Application Form. Upload a single pdf file containing the two pages scanned together, or a zip file that has separate pdf files for each page. Ensure that all additional information required therein has been filled legibly by hand, and photograph and signature are present.

  2. Photograph: Upload a soft copy of a professionally taken passport-sized photograph in JPEG and JPG format ONLY. Selfies using smartphones are NOT preferred . When you upload a photo you will be provided with a pop up that allows you to resize the photo and submit it in 150px by 150px size. You are required to use the same for photo submission.

  3. Degree Certificate(s): Upload scans of your 10th, 10+2 and degree / diploma certificates that include your name. Upload all documents as a multi-page pdf or a zip file.

  4. Proof of Date of Birth: Upload a photocopy of your 10th Pass/ Matriculation/ Secondary School Leaving Certificate/ Marksheet clearly stating your date of birth.

  5. Marksheet(s): Upload academic marksheets for all years/ semesters of all your 10th, 10+2 and degree/ diploma examinations. Upload all marksheets together as a multi-page pdf, or a zip file.
