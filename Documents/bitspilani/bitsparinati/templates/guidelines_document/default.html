1. Application Form: This implies pages 1 & 2 of the downloaded pdf of the Application Form. Upload a single pdf file containing the two pages scanned together, or a zip file that has separate pdf files for each page. Ensure that all additional information required therein has been filled legibly by hand, and photograph and signature are present. Upload .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  2. Photograph: Upload a soft copy of a professionally taken passport-sized photograph in JPEG and JPG format ONLY. Selfies using smartphones are NOT preferred . When you upload a photo you will be provided with a pop up that allows you to resize the photo and submit it in 150px by 150px size. You are required to use the same for photo submission.

  3. Employer Consent Form: Upload scanned copy of page 3 of the downloaded pdf of the Application Form. Ensure that all additional information required therein has been filled legibly by hand, and signatures and company stamp are present. Upload .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  4. Mentor Consent Form: Upload scanned copy of page 4 of the downloaded pdf of the Application. Ensure that all additional information required therein has been filled legibly by hand, and signatures are present. Upload .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  5. Degree/ Diploma Certificate(s): Upload scanned copy of Diploma/ BE/ B.Tech./ M.Sc./ MBA/ AMIE/ BITS Pilani B.S. or equivalent certificate that includes your name as it is spelt. Upload all certificates together as a multi-page .PDF, or a .ZIP file. Ensure they are Self-Attested.

  6. Proof of Date of Birth: Upload a photocopy of your 10th Pass/ Matriculation/ Secondary School Leaving Certificate/ Marksheet clearly stating your date of birth. Upload .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  7. Marksheet(s): Upload academic marksheets for all years/ semesters of all your degree/ diploma examinations. Upload all marksheets together as a multi-page .PDF, or a .ZIP file. Ensure they are Self-Attested.

  8. Proof of Work Experience: For the current organisation, please upload a work experience certificate issued by the company HR. For previous organisations, you may upload work experience certificates if available, or else submit the Appointment Letter along with the Relieving Letter. Upload all work experience certificates as a multi-page .PDF, or a .ZIP file.

  9. Company Brochure: Upload latest brochure giving detailed profile of the current employing organisation. In case the brochure is not available, upload copy of the company profile as given on the company website. Upload .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  10. Career Objective: Upload scanned copy of a brief write-up on your career objectives in applying to this programme. Upload .DOC, .DOCX, .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  11. Mentor Resume & Degree Certificate: Upload copy of detailed resume of your proposed Mentor along with an attested photocopy of his/ her highest degree certificate. Upload a multi-page .PDF, or a .ZIP file.

  12. Letter of Recommendation (only applicable for employees of educational institutions): If you are currently working for a University or an educational institution, upload a letter of recommendation from the head of the institution, such as Vice Chancellor or Registrar of the University. Only those candidate who work with educational institutions are required to submit this document. Upload .PDF or .ZIP file only.

  13. Equivalency Certificate: If you hold a degree/ diploma from a non-Indian University, please provide a certificate of equivalency in .PDF format.

  14. Change of Name Proof: If you have changed your name, and if your name in the application form differs from your name as spelt in your educational certificates, marksheets or other supporting documents, please provide a proof of name change in .PDF format.
