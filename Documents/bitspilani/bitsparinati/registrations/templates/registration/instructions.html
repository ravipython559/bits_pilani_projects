Work Integrated Learning Programs Application Guidelines Important Guidelines for Application Submission
  • Fill the form below with information as required in each section.After completion, check all information for accuracy and then press 'Submit'.
  • After submission,do remember to download a copy of the application form to your computer, and take a printout of the same.
  • After submission, you will see a link to pay online the application fee of INR 1500.You can use this link to pay online through netbanking/Debit Card/ Credit Card. Alternatively, you may choose to send the application fee later through a challan/Demand Draft.
  • The downloaded form will include the Employer Consent Form and Mentor Consent Form that must be completed and attached to your printed Application form. Also included is a checklist of supporting documents such as academic certificates and proof of work experience, which must be attached by a Gazetted Officer or your employer organization's HR and attached to your Application Form.
  • The printed Application Form, along with Employer Consent Form.Checklist of documents, and copies of supporting documents should be dispatched to the following address, and must reach before Dec 9,2015
